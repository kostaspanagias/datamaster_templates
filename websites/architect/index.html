About Green Houses

Our Philosophy & History

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Green Houses was born from a desire to merge cutting-edge architectural design with uncompromising sustainability. We believe that beautiful homes shouldn't cost the earth. For over a decade, we've been dedicated to pioneering new methods in green building.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to design and build homes that are healthy for both people and the planet. We are committed to using locally-sourced, renewable materials, minimizing construction waste, and creating spaces that are not only energy-efficient but also deeply connected to the natural environment.
